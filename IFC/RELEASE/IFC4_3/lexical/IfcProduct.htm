Een IfcProduct bevindt zich op een specifieke locatie in de ruimte als er een geometrische representatie aan is toegewezen. De ObjectPlacement attribuut stelt het coördinatensysteem vast waarin alle punten en richtingen die worden gebruikt door de geometrische representatie-items onder representatie zijn opgericht. De representatie wordt geleverd door een IfcProductDefinitionShape die ofwel een geometrische vormrepresentatie is, of een topologie representatie (met of zonder onderliggende geometrie van de topologische elementen).

🇵🇹 Produto
O IfcProduct é uma representação abstrata de qualquer objeto relacionada com um contexto geométrico ou espacial. Um IfcProduct ocorre numa localização específica no espaço se lhe for atribuída uma representação geométrica. Pode ser colocado relativamente a outros produtos, mas, em última análise, relativamente ao sistema de coordenadas do projeto. O atributo ObjectPlacement estabelece o sistema de coordenadas em que se baseiam todos os pontos e direcções utilizados pelos elementos de representação geométrica em Representation. A Representation é fornecida por uma IfcProductDefinitionShape que pode ser uma representação de forma geométrica ou uma representação topológica (com ou sem geometria subjacente dos elementos topológicos).

🇷🇴 Produs
IfcProduct este o reprezentare abstractă a oricărui obiect care se referă la un context geometric sau spaţial. O IfcProduct are loc într-o locaţie specifică în spaţiu dacă are o reprezentare geometrică atribuită. Ea poate fi plasată relativ la alte produse, dar, în cele din urmă, în raport cu sistemul de coordonare a proiectului. Representation este asigurată de IfcProductDefinitionShape fiind fie o reprezentare a formei geometrice, sau o reprezentare a topologiei (cu sau fără geometria subiacentă a elementelor topologice).

IfcProduct, geometrik veya mekânsal bir bağlama ilişkin herhangi bir nesnenin soyut bir temsilidir. Bir IfcProduct, atanmış bir geometrik temsile sahipse, uzayda belirli bir konumda gerçekleşir. Diğer ürünlere göre görece olarak yerleştirilebilir, fakat nihai olarak projenin koordinat sistemine bağlıdır. ObjectPlacement özelliği, geometrik temsil öğeleri tarafından kullanılan tüm noktaların ve yönlerin temellendirildiği koordinat sistemini belirler. Temsil, IfcProductDefinitionShape tarafından sağlanır ve bu temsil, ya geometrik şekil temsili ya da topoloji temsili (altta yatan geometri olsun veya olmasın topolojik öğelerin) olabilir.

5.1.3.10.1 Semantic definition

The IfcProduct is an abstract representation of any object that relates to a geometric or spatial context. An IfcProduct occurs at a specific location in space if it has a geometric representation assigned. It can be placed relatively to other products, but ultimately relative to the project coordinate system. The ObjectPlacement attribute establishes the coordinate system in which all points and directions used by the geometric representation items under Representation are founded. The Representation is provided by an IfcProductDefinitionShape being either a geometric shape representation, or a topology representation (with or without underlying geometry of the topological items).

Products include manufactured, supplied or created objects (referred to as elements) for incorporation into an AEC/FM project. This also includes objects that are created indirectly by other products, as spaces are defined by bounding elements. Products can be designated for permanent use or temporary use, an example of the latter is formwork. Products are defined by their properties and representations.

In addition to physical products (covered by the subtype IfcElement) and spatial items (covered by the subtype IfcSpatialElement) the IfcProduct also includes non-physical items, that relate to a geometric or spatial context, such as grid, port, annotation, structural actions, etc.

Any instance of IfcProduct defines a particular occurrence of a product. The common type information, that relates to many similar (or identical) occurrences of IfcProduct, is handled by the IfcTypeProduct (and its subtypes), assigned to one or many occurrences of IfcProduct by using the objectified relationship IfcRelDefinesByType. The IfcTypeProduct may provide, in addition to common properties, also a common geometric representation for all occurrences.

NOTE See IfcTypeProduct for how to use a common geometric representation and IfcRelDefinesByType for using and overriding common properties.

On a generic level products can be assigned to processes, controls, resources, project by using the relationship objects that refer to the corresponding object:

  • Having a control applied: assigned using IfcRelAssignsToControl linking the IfcProduct to an IfcControl
  • Being assigned to a process: assigned using IfcRelAssignsToProcess linking the IfcProduct to an IfcProcess
  • Being assigned to a resource: assigned using IfcRelAssignsToResource linking the IfcProduct to an IfcResource
EXAMPLE An example of the control relationship is the assignment of a performance history to a distribution element. An example of process assignment relationship is the assignment of products like wall, slab, and column to a construction task for construction planning. An example of resource assignment relationship is the assignment of products to a construction resource that consumes the product.

5.1.3.10.5 Formal representation 

ENTITY IfcProduct
ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF
(IfcAnnotation
,IfcElement
,IfcLinearElement
,IfcPort
,IfcPositioningElement
,IfcSpatialElement
,IfcStructuralActivity
,IfcStructuralItem))
SUBTYPE OF (IfcObject);
ObjectPlacement : OPTIONAL IfcObjectPlacement;
Representation : OPTIONAL IfcProductRepresentation;
INVERSE
PositionedRelativeTo : SET [0:?] OF IfcRelPositions FOR RelatedProducts;
ReferencedBy : SET [0:?] OF IfcRelAssignsToProduct FOR RelatingProduct;
ReferencedInStructures : SET [0:?] OF IfcRelReferencedInSpatialStructure FOR RelatedElements;
WHERE
PlacementForShapeRepresentation : (EXISTS(Representation) AND EXISTS(ObjectPlacement))
OR (EXISTS(Representation) AND
(SIZEOF(QUERY(temp <* Representation.Representations | 'IFC4X3_ADD2.IFCSHAPEREPRESENTATION' IN TYPEOF(temp))) = 0))
OR (NOT(EXISTS(Representation)));
END_ENTITY;
