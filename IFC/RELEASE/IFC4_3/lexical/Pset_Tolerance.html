5.4.4.29.1 Semantic definition

Properties expressing the tolerance relating to locating and shaping of an intended element or feature. Range diameters are non-negative describing a linear, rectangular or boxed region .

5.4.4.29.2 Applicable entities

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE The property sets defined by this IfcPropertySetTemplate can be assigned to subtypes of IfcTypeObject and can be overridden by a property set with same name at subtypes of IfcObject.
