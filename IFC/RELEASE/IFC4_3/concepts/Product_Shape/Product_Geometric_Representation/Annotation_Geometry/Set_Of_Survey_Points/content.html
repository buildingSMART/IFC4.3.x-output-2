4.1.7.1.2.3 Set Of Survey Points

Geometry representation of a set of survey points. They can be 2D points or 3D points. The set of points may not form a closed surface or a survey line.

NOTE When a single IfcAnnotation is represented by multiple survey points, it is not possible to associate properties to the single geometrical points of the set, but only to the whole annotation.
