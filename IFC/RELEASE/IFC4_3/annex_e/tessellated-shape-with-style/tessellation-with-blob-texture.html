E.9.1 - Tessellation With Blob Texture

Example overview

﻿The various examples demonstrate the use of tessellated items with indexed color map and with texturing.

﻿The tessellated shape representation includes an indexed texture coordinate list, providing texture vertices for each face, along with a texture embedded within the file encoded in PNG format. Figure A shows a cylinder with faces having specific texture coordinates.
