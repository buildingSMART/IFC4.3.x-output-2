E.9.2 - Tessellation With Image Texture

Example overview

﻿The various examples demonstrate the use of tessellated items with indexed color map and with texturing.

﻿The tessellated shape representation includes an indexed texture coordinate list, providing texture vertices for each face, along with a texture referenced by URL. Figure A shows a cylinder with faces having specific texture coordinates. The texture for use in this model is provided in Figure B.
